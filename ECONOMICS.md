# ECONOMICS.md

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assuming:
- Average deal size: $8,000 in credits purchased (a 15-person startup spending $500/month on AI APIs buys ~16 months of credits upfront for the discount)
- Credex margin: ~12% on credits (sourced from overforecast inventory)
- Gross profit per deal: **$960**
- Average customer lifetime (repurchases): 2.2× (companies stay on AI tools)
- **LTV: ~$2,100 per converted customer**

## Conversion Funnel

```
Visitors to SpendScope       100%
Audit completed              ~35%   (strong CTAs, fast form)
Email captured               ~12%   (value-first, gate after results)
High-savings flag (>$500/mo) ~30%   (of audits completed)
Credex CTA clicked           ~18%   (of high-savings audits)
Consultation booked          ~40%   (of CTA clicks)
Deal closed                  ~25%   (of consultations)
```

**Math:**
- 1,000 visitors
- 350 audits completed
- 42 emails captured
- ~105 high-savings audits
- ~19 CTA clicks
- ~7.5 consultations booked
- ~1.9 deals closed

**Revenue per 1,000 visitors:** 1.9 × $960 = **$1,824 gross profit** per 1,000 visitors.

At a Show HN launch yielding 5,000 visitors → ~$9,120 gross profit from first event alone.

## What Needs to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

**Target:** $1M ARR = ~$83k/month in recurring credit sales = ~87 new customers/month (at $8k ACV).

| Milestone | Month | Customers | Monthly Visitors Needed |
|-----------|-------|-----------|------------------------|
| Launch + HN | 1 | 10 | 5,000 (one-time burst) |
| Organic SEO kicking in | 6 | 40/mo | 20,000/mo |
| Cursor co-marketing live | 9 | 60/mo | 30,000/mo |
| Referral loop active | 12 | 80/mo | 40,000/mo |
| $1M ARR | 18 | 87/mo | 45,000/mo |

**What has to be true:**
1. The audit tool gets to 2,000+ audits/week by month 6 — requires at least 2 distribution events (HN, Product Hunt) plus referral sharing working (10%+ of audits shared).
2. Audit → consultation conversion rate holds at ≥5% of all audits (not just high-savings) as the funnel scales — requires Credex's sales team to be responsive.
3. Average deal size grows to $12k as customers understand the product — requires a renewal/upsell motion.
4. Cursor co-marketing is live by month 9 — the highest-leverage distribution lever.

**Rough inputs, but the math closes:** The unit economics are strong enough that the question is distribution volume, not margin. Getting to 45k visitors/month in 18 months via organic + partnerships is achievable if the tool genuinely saves people money (which it does).
