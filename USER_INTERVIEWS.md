> **CRITICAL — READ BEFORE SUBMITTING:**
> These interviews MUST be real. The assignment says fabricated interviews are an instant reject. You need to talk to 3 actual humans this week.
>
> **Who to contact:**
> - Friends or classmates running side projects or startups
> - Founders or EMs on Indie Hackers, Twitter/X, LinkedIn
> - Cold DM on Twitter: "Hey, building an AI cost audit tool — could I ask you 5 questions about your current AI tool stack? 10 mins, happy to share results when it's live"
> - Your college network — alumni in engineering/startup roles
>
> **Format for each interview (10–15 min):**
> 1. What AI tools do you pay for and roughly what do you spend?
> 2. How do you decide which plan to be on?
> 3. Have you ever felt like you might be overpaying? What did you do about it?
> 4. If there was a free tool that told you exactly where you could cut, would you use it? What would make you trust it?
> 5. What would make you share a tool like this with a colleague?
