## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup required to see your results. We ask for your email after you've seen the audit — never before.

**Q: How do you make money if this is free?**
A: SpendScope is built by Credex, which sells discounted AI infrastructure credits (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and more) sourced from companies that overforecast. If your audit shows significant savings, we'll offer a Credex consultation. No obligation.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
A: The audit engine uses official pricing from each vendor's pricing page (verified and dated in our source file). Estimates are conservative — we'd rather understate savings than oversell. Always verify with your vendor before making changes.

**Q: Do you store my spending data?**
A: Yes — your audit is saved to generate a shareable link. We store tool names and spend amounts, not personal information. Your email is stored separately and never included in the public share URL.

**Q: What tools are supported?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude (all plans), ChatGPT (all plans), Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. We update pricing weekly. If you use a tool that's not listed, run the audit for what's supported — partial audits are still useful.
